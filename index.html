About This Hamlet

The Strange Mole Show has been calling out corruption, Brexit lies, and fascism since 2018. Written by Ian "The Dude" Gilson, performed with Chris Doc Strange and Chrissie Grech.

We predicted Brexit corruption, Tory collapse, and the current political hellscape back in 2020. We were right about everything. Now we're here to keep fighting.
